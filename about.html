About Me

Sally H

A food lover that currently resides in London, having moved from Hong Kong a few years ago. Food has always been a love language in my culture and I believe cooking brings people together, heals and is my go-to form of self-care. There is just something about chopping those onions into perfectly diced cubes...or that first sip of steaming hot soup that makes you go ahhhhh...

Cooking should be simple, and for those that go for elaborate recipes (as I sometimes do) more power to you, but I want to make cooking seem less scary and provide another simple way for people to take care of themselves.

I hope you find a recipe that you enjoy here!
